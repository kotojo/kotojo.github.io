LeanMeanEats

LeanMeanEats is for gathering all your daily food intake nutritional information into one place. You make recipes by getting all the nutritional information for each ingredient, it then takes the nutritional information from the USDA's nutritional information API, and it is then graphed for each major nutrient based off a 2000 calorie diet.

FlashStack

FlashStack is for creating a platform for practicing flashcards digitally. You are able to create flashcards and save them into decks, which then can be practiced altogether in their deck.

PieceAPC

PieceAPC is for people to be able to create personal computer builds, show them to the user base, and be rated on to find out the quality of their builds. You could search through Newegg's computer parts data to find parts, then add them all together into one build, which would tell you the cost of the total build. All computers were able to be seen by the entire user base, searched by rating or price, and rated upon with an upvote or downvote.
